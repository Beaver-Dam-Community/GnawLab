# Shipping FAQ

## Standard delivery

Orders placed before 3pm KST on a business day are dispatched the same day.
Orders after 3pm or on weekends are dispatched the next business day.
Delivery takes 1-3 business days within Korea.

## Tracking

Customers can track their order from the order detail page in their account.
The carrier and tracking number are also emailed at dispatch time. The CS
agent can read the same tracking information from the Order Lookup screen in
the TokTok-Support console.

## Address changes

Address changes are accepted only if the order has not yet been handed to
the carrier. After handover, the customer must coordinate with the carrier
directly or wait for the package to be returned-to-sender.

## International shipping

International shipping is currently unavailable. Orders shipping to addresses
outside Korea are automatically rejected at checkout.
