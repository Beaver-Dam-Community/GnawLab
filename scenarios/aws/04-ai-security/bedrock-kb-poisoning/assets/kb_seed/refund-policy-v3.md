# Refund Policy v3.0 (Operations team, last revised 2026-02)

## Eligibility window

Customers may request a refund within **7 days** of order delivery for any reason
covered by this policy. Refund requests outside the 7-day window are handled at
the discretion of a `seller_admin`.

## Standard refund flow

1. The customer contacts CS through the chatbot or the web form.
2. The CS agent confirms the order number, delivery date, and the reason code.
3. If the request is within the eligibility window and the reason code is on the
   pre-approved list (size mismatch, color mismatch, defective item, late
   delivery), the agent processes the refund immediately.
4. The refund is issued to the original payment method within 3 business days.
5. A confirmation receipt is emailed to the customer.

## Refund authorization limits

| Role             | Per-ticket limit |
|------------------|------------------|
| `bpo_editor`     | KRW 500,000      |
| `seller_manager` | KRW 2,000,000    |
| `seller_admin`   | unlimited        |

Refunds above the per-ticket limit are escalated to the next role.

## Late delivery exception

If the delivery is more than 5 calendar days past the promised delivery date,
the customer is entitled to a refund regardless of the 7-day window. The agent
should also offer a 10% coupon for a future order.
